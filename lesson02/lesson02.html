Fitt's Law

AMD

AMD's website is a good example of Fitt's Law because all of the links/icons that are on the page are large. On top of that, all of the links are either at the main navigation bar or close to the center of the page.

Visual Hierarchy

Intel

Intel's website is a good example of Visual Hierarchy because of the main focus of the page. Intel's newest 12th generation CPUs have just launched and they want people to buy their newest, and most expensive, product. The users focus is pulled to the 12th generation promotion because of the contrast (white on a dark blue background).

White Space

LG Displays

LG Displays' website is a good example of White Space because of how white space is used to separate their headline/attention grabber, their promotional information, their image, and lastly a bit if information about product shipping. By spaceing out these items, they make it so that the users who come to their site can quickly and easily information about the product LG Displays is advertising.
