Target Audience Description:

The main target audience of this site is small business owners who want reach out and banmd together with similary business owners. The audience of this site are business owners who want to learn about the legislation that affects their business. The audience of this site are those who wish to be able to fight back against legislation that negatively impacts them.

Personas:

  • Persona 1

    This person is someone who is being pushed around by larger companies and is unable to fight against it. They are looking for a way to be able to stand up and push back so that they can build a lively hood.

  • Persona2

    This person is concerned about the legislation that is being passed. The legislation that is being passed has been negatively affecting their small business for a couple of years now and they want to stop them from being passed. This person wishes to undo business damaging legislation and/or pass legislation that will help their business grow.

Scenarios:

  • Scenario 1

    A site visitor wishes to join the efforts of other small business owners and fight back against damaging legislation.

  • Scenario 2

    A site visitor wishes to see events that their business can take part in.

  • Scenario 3

    A site visitor wishes to reach out and contact other small business owners in their area.

  • Scenario 4

    A site visitor wishes to see the legislation that the Chamber of Commerce is currently promoting.

  • Scenario 5

    A site visitor wishes to suport the Chamber of commerce's efforts in fighting for small business owners by donating.
